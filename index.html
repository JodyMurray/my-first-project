Biography

Robin Hobb, an acclaimed American Fantasy novelist, is known for her remarkable Realm of the Elderlings series. Writing extensively under the name Megan Lindholm, her iconic works include The Farseer Trilogy, celebrating its 25th birthday with a lavish illustrated edition. Other notable works encompass The Liveship Traders Trilogy, The Tawny Many Trilogy, The Rain Wild Chronicles, and The Fitz and the Fool Trilogy. Residing in the Pacific Northwest, she shares her 14-acre pocket farm near Roy with her family, two rescued Belgian Malinois, Ginger and Molly, fifteen chickens, a gander and 8 ducks, numerous volunteers, mallards, and a cat named Little Black Dog.
